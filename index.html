Abby's Past, Present, and Future

Past

I was born on October 24, 2000 in Salt Lake City, Utah. After that, I lived in a small town just north of there for twelve years. I started out in a small house in Kaysville, Utah where I learned to walk, talk, eat, dance, play, and went to my first years of school. My grandparents lived only a few minutes away so my grandma signed me up for dance class and she and my grandpa would take me to lunch afterwards. I sometimes would just go to play with their dogs. We would also go to their house for Sunday dinners, birthdays and holidays. When I was nine, I moved across town which seemed like the worst thing ever since I had to change elementary schools. When I turned ten, I learned how to ski and started training dogs. Three years later, we moved again. This time, we moved across the country to Charlotte, North Carolina. It was difficult for me, but I ended up loving it there too.

Memories of My Past:

  1. My first dance class
  2. Going to the waterpark near my house
  3. Learning to ski
  4. Training and running dog agility
